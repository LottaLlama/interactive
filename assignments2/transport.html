How to get to
Underthebed



From Closetfloor:

Head out of Closetfloor to the west
Curve south and pass through Underthecouch
Traverse over Tissuepile
Turn toward north to reach Underthebed

From Inthecouch:

Climb over Inthecouch edge or drop down to Underthebed
Head west and traverse over Tissuepile
Turn toward north to reach Underthebed

From Underthefridge:

Crawl out of Underthefridge and head west across the White Plains
Head up the ridge to the Brown Linelands
Turn south to cross onto the Carpet Greenway
Make a sharp turn west across the tall beige ridge; watch your footing
Meander through the Tinny Forest toward Underthebed


From Tubdrain:

Traverse Slippery Lakes and turn east to climb up Large Valley
Drop down on the other side of Large Valley onto the Rug Grayway
Cross the Cold Plains
Head up the ridge to the Brown Linelands and immediately head south
Enter the Carpet Greenway and make a sharp turn west across the tall beige ridge
Meander through the Tinny Forest toward Underthebed

From Sinkdrain:

Walk across the Puddle and climb up Small Valley
Scale down White Wood Canyon and turn east to cross the Cold Plains
Head up the ridge to the Brown Linelands and immediately head south
Enter the Carpet Greenway and make a sharp turn west across the tall beige ridge
